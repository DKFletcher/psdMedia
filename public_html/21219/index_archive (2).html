Hello and thank you for taking the time to complete this assessment. You will have been navigated here by a link in an email you have recieved. This assessment regards only the learner indicated in the email and has been requested by Ladywell School in order that we can continue to support the learner.

Everything needs to be anonymous so no real names in your work please. Just the class subject name e.g. "English" or "PSA" etc in the text box indicated.

Bellow this instruction is a TELI. It consists of 24 statements and we are asking you to use your experience of the learner to form a view if that statement is true (YES button), false (NO button), and sometimes true (SOMETIMES button). At the left side of the buttons is a text box for you to make any additional comments regarding your view (if you feel you need to). There will be a new comments box for every statement.

Once you have formed your view, and have clicked on the appropriate button, the assessment will move onto the next statement.

If you need to go back to review or revise your views click onto the "TELI" graphic in the bottom right and then use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate through your views.

When you have finished this assessment, click the "SEND" button and you are done.

This assessment is not supposed to take a long time or be too onerous for you to do. We do appreciate that this process may be new for you and may take a bit longer because you are not used to it, so thank you again for taking the time. If you any questions then give Ladywell a call and we will try our best to answer them.
