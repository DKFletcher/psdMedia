Instructions

level1

  • Look down the list, and click on any items that describe you
  • Click in the middle of the wheel to move round to the next list
  • Repeat until you have finished all seven lists

level2

  • Click on "level2"
  • Read all the descriptions and rate how well that list describes you in school, from 0 to 10
  • Click on top of the number you chose, using the 0 to 10 scale
  • Move round to the next list by clicking the middle of the wheel
  • Repeat until you have finished all seven lists

level3

  • Click on "level3"
  • Choose 2 or 3 words from the list that best describe what you most like about you. Click onto "most like about me" and then click on your chosen descriptions, which now should now be highlighted green.
  • Choose 2 or 3 words that best describe what you least like about you. Click onto "least like about me" and then click on your chosen descriptions, which now should now be highlighted yellow.
  • Type your name in the box
  • Click "my stances" and save your file
