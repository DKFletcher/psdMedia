Instructions

level1

Click each description three times to find the one that BEST DESCRIBES YOU. Each description has a comments section underneath for you to use, if you want to. When you have chosen your descriptions, and they have all turned green, turn the page by pressing the middle of the wheel. Keep going until the progress bar is full.

level2

Start at question 1. There is a question at the top of the box, when you have answered the question, press the middle of the wheel to move to the next question.

level3

Fill in your details. If you have finished all the descriptions then you will be able to click on "my chart" to make your Energy for Learning chart. Save your file.
