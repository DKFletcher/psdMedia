Parents

Parents styleFile

All parents want to be as good a parent as they can be. We all fall short of perfection. The Parent Profile lets you think about your parenting style. It asks you to choose certain descriptions that best fit you. You are then asked to rate yourself on how well each of nine columns describes you. You end up with a chart that shows your profile that reflects what you feel are your strengths and issues. After you have completed the profile, it will be useful to check it out with others.




Parenting Styles Indicator (PSI)

All parents want to be as good a parent as they can be. We all fall short of perfection. The Parenting Indicator helps you to think through how you relate to your children. It invites you to choose statements that best describe what you are like at home. You end up with a chart that helps you work out where you currently are on four dimensions of parenting. After you have completed the profile, it will be useful to check it out with others.
