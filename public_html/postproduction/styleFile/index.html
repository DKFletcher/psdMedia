Instructions

level1

Look down the list, and click on any items that describe you. Click in the middle of the wheel to move round to the next list. Repeat until you have finished all seven lists.

level2

Read all the descriptions and rate how well that list describes you in school, from 0 to 10. Click on top of the number you chose, using the 0 to 10 scale. Move round to the next list by clicking the middle of the wheel. Repeat until you have finished all seven lists.

level3

Choose 2 or 3 words from your descriptions that best describe what you most like about you. Click on "most like about me" and then click on your chosen descriptions, (now highlighted green).
If there are other descriptions that better describe what you most like about yourself that don't appear on your list, you can put them in the box top right.

Choose 2 or 3 words that best describe what you least like about you. Click on "least like about me" and then click on your chosen descriptions, (now highlighted yellow).
If there are other descriptions that better describe what you least like about yourself that don't appear on your list, you can put them in the box bottom right.

Choose 1 or 2 words that best describe the real you. Click on "the real me" and then click on your chosen descriptions, which now should be highlighted blue.

level4

Type your name in the box. Click "my stances" and save your file.
