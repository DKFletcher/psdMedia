Hello, and welcome to whatMotivatesLearning.com

What you will find on this site is the result of 15 years of research, reflection and consultation, of individuals and teams, learners of all ages and stages, parents and carers, teachers, educational psychologists and lecturers.

All adding to the drive, given focus, inspiration and motivation through our ongoing effort. We are concerned with one of the most fundamental questions surrounding education, what motivates learning?




What motivates learning is a question that has as many answers as there are people and situations. You will find that the whatMotivatesLearning toolset will help you to the truth of this complicated question as it ebbs and flows around you. There are six individual tools that are freely available for any learner, teacher, or parent to use. Each tool has one of two basic formats.

There is other online content that you may want to buy (the assembly, zero, and share) and these can be viewed in more detail from the resources page. Please feel free to email us at sales@whatMotivatesLearning.com to enquire further.

Similarly, Alan McLean has written some wonderful books that accompany the content, and if you want to further develop your skills using these tools, or understand their evolution then reading his books are a must.
