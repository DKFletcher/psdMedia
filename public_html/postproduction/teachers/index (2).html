Teachers

styleFile

Teacher are always looking for opportunities to reflect on their teaching. The styleFile lets you think about your teaching style and how you come across to your pupils, how you relate to them and make them feel. It asks you to describe your teaching style by choosing certain descriptions that best fit you. You are then asked to rate yourself on how well each of nine columns describes you. You end up with a chart that shows your style profile. After you have completed the profile, it will be useful to invite other people to complete a profile of you, a colleague or a class of young people.




Teaching Style Indicator (TSI)

As a teacher you will be looking for opportunities to reflect on your teaching. The Teaching Style Indicator gives you a chance to think about your teaching style and how you come across to your pupils, how you relate to them and make them feel. It invites you to choose statements that best describe what you are like in class. You end up with a chart that helps you think through where you are on four dimensions of teaching. After you have completed the profile, it will be useful to invite other people to complete a profile of you, a colleague or a class of young people.
