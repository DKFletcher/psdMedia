stanceGlance

stanceGlance invites you to describe yourself by choosing certain descriptions that best fit you. You then rate yourself on how well each of nine columns describes you. You end up with a chart that shows you where you are putting most of your energies. Stances help us make sense of how we think and feel about ourselves and our current intentions. Stances are like passing moods, they change all the time. Stances fit all times, good and bad, but some are defensive and we can get stuck in them.
