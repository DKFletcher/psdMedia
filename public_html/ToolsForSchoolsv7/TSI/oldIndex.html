Instructions

level1

  • Explore the descriptions by clicking on top of each till you find one that BEST DESCRIBES YOUR TEACHER
  • Each description has a comments section underneath for you to use
  • When you have chosen your descriptions, and they have all turned green, turn the page by pressing the middle of the wheel
  • Keep going until the progress bar is full

level2

  • Click on "level2"
  • There is a question at the top of the box, when you have answered the question, press the middle of the wheel to move to the next question

level3

  • Click on "level3"
  • Fill in your details
  • If you have finished all the descriptions then you will be able to click on "my chart" to make your Teaching Styles chart
  • Save your file
